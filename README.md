## 🌟 Overview  
This project presents a **high-impact Tableau dashboard** designed to analyze air quality trends across major Indian cities.

It transforms raw AQI data into **actionable insights** by uncovering pollution patterns, seasonal variations, and high-risk regions through advanced visualization and forecasting techniques.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective  
To analyze air quality data and provide insights into:

- 🌫️ Pollution trends across cities  
- 📍 Identification of high-risk (worst AQI) locations  
- 📅 Monthly and seasonal AQI variations  
- 📊 Distribution of AQI categories (Good, Moderate, Poor, Severe)  
- 🔮 Future AQI trends using forecasting  

---

## 🧠 Analytical Approach  

- 📊 Data aggregation and cleaning  
- 📈 Trend analysis of AQI over time  
- 🌍 City-wise comparison of air quality  
- 🔍 Identification of pollution hotspots  
- 🔮 Forecasting future AQI patterns  
- 🎯 Interactive dashboard design  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights  

- 🌫️ Certain cities consistently experience **high AQI levels (pollution hotspots)**  
- 📍 Pune identified as a **critical air quality zone**  
- 📈 AQI shows strong **seasonal variation trends**  
- ⚠️ Majority of AQI falls under **Moderate to Poor categories**  
- 🔮 Forecast indicates **potential future pollution risks**
